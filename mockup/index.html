Active Beacon Design Aesthetics

  • 🚗

    Side Silhouette

    Flat, solid side-view shapes of transportation assets and keys, keeping detailed geometry minimal.

    Signal Wave Focus

    Radiating arcs representing Bluetooth beacons and active location mapping grids.

  • 🎨

    Warm Pastel Theme

    Elegant peach, lavender, soft mint, rose, and light-blue backdrops offering organic contrast.

  • 📱

    Dynamic Scaling

    Clean SVG formatting with no filters or shadows, optimizing performance on high-resolution displays.
